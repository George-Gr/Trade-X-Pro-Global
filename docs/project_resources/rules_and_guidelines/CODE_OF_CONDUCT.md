## 4. Enforcement

### 4.1 Reporting Process
If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:

1. **Document**: Note the specific behavior, date, time, and people involved
2. **Report**: Contact the Code of Conduct team at conduct@trade-x-pro.dev with:
   - Description of incident
   - Participants involved (if comfortable sharing)
   - Links to relevant discussions (GitHub issues, PRs, etc.)
   - Any supporting screenshots or documentation

3. **Confidentiality**: Reports are handled confidentially. We will not publicly identify reporters without consent

### 4.2 Investigation
- Response within 48 hours
- Confidential investigation by Code of Conduct committee
- Both parties given opportunity to share perspective
- Investigation completed within 2 weeks

### 4.3 Resolution

**Warning**: For minor first-time violations:
- Private communication about expectations
- Opportunity to correct behavior
- No escalation if resolved quickly

**Temporary Suspension**: For repeated or serious violations:
- Suspension from repository access: 1-4 weeks
- Suspension from community channels: 1-4 weeks
- Clear expectations for reinstatement

**Permanent Ban**: For severe or repeated violations:
- Permanent removal from all project spaces
- No future contribution access
- Public announcement (if appropriate)

### 4.4 Appeals
- Appeals reviewed by Code of Conduct committee chair
- Appeal must be submitted within 2 weeks of enforcement action
- Decision on appeal within 1 week
